Design Brief – KrishiPath Company Dashboard (MVP)
Project Overview

Design a world-class Company Dashboard for KrishiPath, an agricultural marketing platform where pesticide, fertilizer, seed, and agri companies can create educational campaigns, reward farmers for engaging with content, and receive callback requests from interested farmers.

This is NOT a traditional advertising platform like Facebook Ads.

The goal is to create a simple, trustworthy, transparent, and agriculture-focused marketing dashboard that even a first-time company user can understand within a few minutes.

The UI should feel modern, premium, professional, and easy to use while remaining optimized for Indian businesses.

Design Philosophy

The dashboard should communicate:

Transparency
Simplicity
Trust
Professionalism
Agriculture-first
Data-driven decisions

Avoid overwhelming users with too much information.

Every screen should answer one question:

"What should the company do next?"

User Experience

The company should complete its first campaign in under five minutes.

Every important action should require the minimum number of clicks.

The interface should always explain what happens next.

Never confuse the user.

Overall Goal

Design an MVP that feels like a premium SaaS product rather than a basic admin panel.

Every screen should make companies feel confident that:

Their money is transparent.
Their campaigns are easy to manage.
Farmer engagement is measurable.
The platform is trustworthy and scalable.

The final design should be polished enough that a real agricultural company would feel comfortable using it from day one.
